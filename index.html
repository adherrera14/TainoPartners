    Taino Partners is an affiliate program that will pay commissions on the revenue share generated by players affiliates bring.

  • Is it Free to Sign Up?

    Yes, signing is completely free, simply click on the register button and fill in the information.

  • How do I start earning?

    Once you sign up your account will be reviewed and approved to start promoting and earning.

    We work with a standard revenue share commission, but we can work out other types of deals case by case.

    All commissions are calculated by the end of each month minus the player’s fees, the commissions will be paid by the middle of the following month.
